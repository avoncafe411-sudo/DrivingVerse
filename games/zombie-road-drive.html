About Zombie Road Drive

Zombie Road Drive combines fast-paced driving with zombie survival action. Your vehicle never stops moving, so every second counts as you dodge wrecked cars, avoid dangerous obstacles, and eliminate waves of hungry zombies. Quick reactions and smart driving are the key to staying alive on the endless highway.

Unlike traditional racing games, every run is different. The farther you travel, the more intense the zombie attacks become. Whether you enjoy driving games, action games, or survival challenges, Zombie Road Drive delivers an exciting arcade experience directly in your browser with no download required.

Game Controls

Left Arrow Key - Move Left
Right Arrow Key - Move Right
Shoot zombies while avoiding collisions.

Tips to Survive Longer

  • Stay near the center of the road whenever possible.
  • Avoid unnecessary crashes because every collision reduces your vehicle's health.
  • Destroy zombies before they get too close.
  • Keep moving and react quickly to new obstacles.
