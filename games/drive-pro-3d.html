About Drive Pro 3D

Drive Pro 3D is a fast-paced arcade driving game where perfect timing is the key to success. Hold to accelerate, release to brake instantly, and carefully weave through busy traffic while avoiding dangerous obstacles. Every level becomes more challenging as your driving skills improve.

Complete levels to earn coins and unlock stylish new vehicles with unique appearances. Whether you enjoy casual driving games or quick reflex challenges, Drive Pro 3D offers smooth gameplay and exciting action that is easy to learn but difficult to master.

Game Controls

Mouse Click / Tap — Hold to Accelerate
Release Mouse / Finger — Brake Instantly

Tips for Better Scores

  • Don't hold the accelerator continuously.
  • Brake early before reaching heavy traffic.
  • Watch moving vehicles carefully before crossing.
  • Collect coins to unlock better-looking cars.
