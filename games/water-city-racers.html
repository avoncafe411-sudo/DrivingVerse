Description

Water City Racers is an exciting 3D racing game where you can drive powerful sports cars across futuristic roads surrounded by water. Race against opponents, perform incredible jumps, collect rewards, unlock new vehicles and enjoy multiple game modes with realistic driving physics. Whether you enjoy fast racing or simply exploring the environment, Water City Racers delivers an action-packed driving experience directly in your browser.

Controls

W A S D — Drive
Shift — Nitro Boost
Space — Handbrake
R — Reset Vehicle
T — Look Behind
