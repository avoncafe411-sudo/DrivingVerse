Speed Racer

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ 4.7 / 5

Speed Racer is an open-source browser racing game where players drive at high speed while avoiding obstacles and aiming for the highest score.

How to Play

Accelerate through the course and avoid obstacles while keeping your speed under control. Use quick reflexes to weave past barriers, collect points, and survive as long as possible.

Controls

Arrow Keys Steer the vehicle and adjust speed.
Space Use boost or brake depending on game mode.

Features

  • Fast-paced open-source racing action.
  • Responsive browser controls for desktop and mobile.
  • Obstacle avoidance and score-based gameplay.
  • Instant play with no downloads required.
