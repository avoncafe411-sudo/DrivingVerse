Description

Offroad Truck Driving Simulator is the ultimate mud truck driving experience. Drive powerful 4x4 trucks across mountains, dirt roads, rivers, and rough terrain. Upgrade your vehicles, conquer difficult obstacles, complete missions, and become the king of off-road trucking.

Controls

W A S D or Arrow Keys - Move Truck
H - Horn
R - Reverse
