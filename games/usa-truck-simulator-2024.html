USA Truck Simulator 2024 puts you behind the wheel of powerful American trucks as you transport cattle across forests, countryside roads and rugged off-road routes. Complete realistic delivery missions, navigate difficult terrain, switch camera views and enjoy an authentic truck driving experience with smooth controls and immersive environments.
