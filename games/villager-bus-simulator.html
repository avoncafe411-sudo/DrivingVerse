Description

Villager Bus Simulator lets you experience the life of a countryside bus driver. Drive through beautiful village roads, pick up passengers from bus stops, safely drop them at their destinations and complete transport missions while enjoying smooth driving physics and realistic bus controls.

Controls

W — Accelerate
S — Brake
R — Reverse
Shift — Gear
C — Change Camera

Game Features

  • Realistic village bus driving.
  • Passenger pickup and drop missions.
  • Smooth driving controls.
  • Multiple camera views.
  • Immersive countryside environment.
  • Play instantly without downloading.
