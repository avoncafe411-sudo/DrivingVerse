DrivingVerse DMCA Policy

DrivingVerse respects copyright and intellectual property rights. We are committed to responding quickly to valid DMCA takedown requests and removing or disabling access to infringing content when appropriate.

Copyright and intellectual property

All content on DrivingVerse is provided for informational and entertainment purposes. If you believe that any content on our site infringes your copyright, please notify us so we can investigate the claim.

Submitting a DMCA notice

To submit a DMCA takedown notice, send a written request to our designated agent at the email address below. Your notice should include:

  • A description of the copyrighted work that you claim has been infringed.
  • Identification of the allegedly infringing material and where it appears on the site.
  • Your contact information, including email address and phone number.
  • A statement that you have a good faith belief the use is not authorized by the copyright owner.
  • A statement under penalty of perjury that the information in the notice is accurate and that you are authorized to act on behalf of the owner.

Contact for takedown requests

Please send DMCA notices to:

contact@drivingverse.com

Investigation and removal

Valid DMCA complaints will be investigated promptly. If we determine that the content infringes copyright, we will remove or disable access to the material as required by law.

We may also notify the user who posted the content and provide an opportunity to submit a counter-notice if they believe the material was removed in error.
